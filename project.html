Welcome to Germaine College, a place where learning meets creativity and every student is encouraged to reach their full potential. Our school prides itself on fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment, where students not only excel academically but also develop important life skills. OUR COURSES

ICT

This course provides students with a solid foundation in information and communication technology, covering topics such as computer systems, networking, software applications, and digital communication. Students will develop practical skills in troubleshooting, programming, and data management, preparing them for careers in the fast-evolving tech industry.

ENGINEERING

This course introduces students to the core principles of engineering, including design processes, problem-solving, materials science, and applied mathematics. Through hands-on projects and teamwork, students will develop technical and critical thinking skills, preparing them for advanced study in fields like civil, mechanical, or electrical engineering.

COSMETOLOGY

This course introduces students to the art and science of cosmetology, covering hair care, skincare, makeup application, and nail techniques. Students will gain hands-on experience, learn about industry tools and products, and develop the creativity, technical skills, and customer service needed for a successful career in the beauty industry.

NURSING

This course provides students with the foundational knowledge and practical skills needed for a career in nursing. Topics include patient care, health assessment, medical terminology, and basic clinical procedures. Emphasis is placed on compassionate care, critical thinking, and professional ethics, preparing students for further study and clinical practice.
